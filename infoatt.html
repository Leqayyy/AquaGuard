Cleaning Event Teams


Surface Cleaning Team


Collects waste from the water surface and shoreline to secure clean areas. They use tools like paddles, mesh nets, and canoes to clean hard-to-reach spots.


Logistics Team


Provides, moves, and sets up all materials needed by the teams. They are also responsible for cleaning tools and snacks.


Diving Team


Finds and removes plastic waste underwater. The logistics team provides the diving suits.


Data Analysis Team


Studies the data and creates heatmaps to show plastic pollution levels. They also combine data from satellites and drones.


First Aid Team


Helps with any accidents during the event and provides basic first aid. They also offer sunscreen and water to participants.


Media and Communication Team


Takes photos and videos of the event. They also share updates on social media and connect with the press.


Environmental Education Team


Teaches people about the environment and the harm caused by plastic pollution. They also introduce the project to visitors.
